## About Me

Bilingual Electronics Engineer (Spanish/English) with a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence and 6+ years of IT experience. I work as a **Senior Technical & AI Ops Engineer**, focused on incident management, platform reliability, and operational observability for cloud-native SaaS platforms.

I troubleshoot distributed systems across Kubernetes workloads, message queues, databases, and internal services. I lead root cause analysis (RCA) for complex incidents, build runbooks, improve monitoring, and drive operational excellence through automation.

Beyond ops, I develop custom applications and open-source tools. My Master's research focused on image steganalysis using CNNs and Vision Transformers.
